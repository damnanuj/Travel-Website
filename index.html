Sikkim Buddha Park

Gently seated in a small tourist town in the southern part of Sikkim, Buddha Park is a must visit place by every traveller. It was constructed on account of the 2550th birth anniversary of Lord Gautama Buddha in 2006.

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal is a five-storey building, and it is the tallest building in the world that has been built without a foundation. It has a curved architecture that leans at an 87 degree angle, and a pyramidal shape which has helped it stay erect for centuries.

Jaishalmer

Jaisalmer means the Hill Fort of Jaisal. Jaisalmer is sometimes called the "Golden City of India" because the yellow sandstone used throughout the architecture of both the fort and the town below, imbues both with a certain golden-yellow light.

Varanashi

The land of Varanasi (Kashi) has been the ultimate pilgrimage spot for Hindus for ages. Hindus believe that one who is graced to die on the land of Varanasi would attain salvation and freedom from the cycle of birth and re-birth.
